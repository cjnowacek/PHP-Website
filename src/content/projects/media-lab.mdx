**Media Lab | 3ds Max Render Farm Pipeline | Python · Windows**

My DevOps and pipeline work at Media Lab centered on a ~130-machine 3ds Max render farm:
keeping every node consistent, and giving artists tooling that just works. Two systems carry
most of that story — one that **deploys** software across the whole farm unattended, and one
that **runs the pipeline** itself, from scene prep to render-farm job submission.

Both are built on standard-library Python and Windows internals (scheduled tasks, UNC shares,
SYSTEM-level execution), packaged and shipped to run reliably on locked-down studio machines
across two facilities.
